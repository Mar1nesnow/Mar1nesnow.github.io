About

I'm a conservation professional with a passion for coastal ecosystems and land stewardship. I thrive at the intersection of field science and community outreach, where hands-on research meets public engagement.

Currently serving as a 2025–2026 AmeriCorps Green Iowa member at Whiterock Conservancy, supporting prescribed burns, invasive species mitigation, and volunteer programs. Before that I worked with Texas Parks and Wildlife, the Darling Marine Center, and Duke University Marine Lab — each shaping how I think about conservation science and communication.

Outside of work, I make art. I believe the best work blurs the line between function and feeling.
